About LOTLOUISCHO STEM CLUB

LOTLOUISCHO STEM CLUB provides comprehensive math preparation focused on strategy, timing, and confidence to help improve students' problem-solving skills in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) and to ace the math portions of the standardized exams taken in the United States. Founded by Louis Sungwoo Cho, a civil engineer in transportation systems engineering and engineering educator, he emphasizes clear explanations, data-driven feedback, and provides efficient study plans. Each lesson emphasizes understanding over memorization so every student can reach their full potential and to flourish in the field of STEM.
