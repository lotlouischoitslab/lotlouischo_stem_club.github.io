About LOTLOUISCHO STEM CLUB

LOTLOUISCHO STEM CLUB provides comprehensive tutorials to help improve students' problem-solving skills in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) and to ace the math portions of the standardized exams such as the SAT, PSAT, ACT, and AP Calculus taken in the U.S. Founded by Louis Sungwoo Cho, a civil engineer in transportation systems engineering, computer scientist, and an engineering educator, he emphasizes clear explanations, data-driven feedback, and provides efficient study plans. Each lesson emphasizes understanding over memorization so every student can reach their full potential in the field of STEM.
